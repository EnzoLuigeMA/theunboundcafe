Our Story
01
The Beginning — March 10, 2014
DOX was born from Ivo's dream. Shortly after, we united our lives in marriage and decided that we would not accept the script others had written for us. We were told our plan was "impossible" and that we should seek "real jobs."
But for those who come from the bottom, "no" is only the starting point for the world.
02
12 Years of Resilience
Over this decade, we faced crises and a pandemic that many declared would be our end. We did not merely survive — we matured. We learned the art of building a company with sweat, honor, and an unshakeable belief in the invisible. Today, DOX stands larger and stronger than ever.
03
International Roots
English was the key that opened global doors for us. Through the school, we crossed oceans and lived experiences that transformed our worldview. Our friendships in the United States taught us values that are now the pillars of our house: the dignity of work, generosity, and the celebration of individual freedom.
04
A Shift in Paradigm
We believe that every great transformation begins in the mind before it manifests in the world. The Unbound House Café is the result of that shift. It is the physical monument of every battle won and every limit we refused to accept.
This place exists because we chose to build it when the world said we couldn't.
05
Your Expansion Hub
We created this "House" to be more than a café. It is a reference point for the English-speaking community — a place to interact, practice, and expand horizons. We bring the curation of our international experiences into your cup.
06
Our Legacy
Under God, we believe that you are exactly what you believe yourself to be. Do not accept limitations. Do not conform. Be indignant against settling for little — and strive to transform your reality.
The Unbound House exists for those who refuse to be bound.
